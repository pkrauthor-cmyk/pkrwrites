Our Story

Built on a Belief

PKR Writes was born from a conviction that the universe is not just something we observe — it is something we feel, question, and experience.

Founded by PKR — a Civil Engineer turned storyteller — the platform reflects a unique journey that bridges logic and creativity, structure and soul. What began as a personal exploration of ideas has evolved into a destination for readers who seek depth, meaning, and inspiration through words.

Every piece of content created here is a bridge between what we know and what we dare to imagine. Between quantum mechanics and the ache of human longing. Between the science of the cosmos and the poetry of being alive.

PKR
The Mind Behind PKR Writes

Thinker. Storyteller. Explorer.

PKR is not merely a writer — but a thinker deeply engaged with cosmology, philosophy, and the unseen forces that shape both the universe and the human soul. A Civil Engineer by training, a storyteller by calling, PKR occupies a rare creative space equally at home discussing quantum mechanics and the ache of human longing.

This unique perspective allows PKR Writes to sit at the intersection of the scientific and the sacred — where every story becomes an experiment in what it means to be fully alive.
